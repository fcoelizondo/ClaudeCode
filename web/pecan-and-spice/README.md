# Pecan & Spice

A redesigned single-page site for Pecan & Spice Kitchen — a home-based
bakery in Bee Cave / Lake Pointe, TX run by Chely Guerra, selling homemade
cakes, chocolate treats, and chili & lime snacks.

Content (copy, products, prices, photos, contact details) was carried over
from the original site at pecanandspice.com; only the design is new.

## Files

- `index.html` — the whole site: hero, category strip, three product
  sections (Cakes & Cookies, Chocolate Treats, Chili & Lime Snacks),
  owner story, and contact.
- `style.css` — warm cream/blue/caramel theme; palette sampled from the
  brand's logo. Responsive, with a mobile nav.
- `app.js` — mobile nav toggle and reveal-on-scroll animations.
- `img/` — product and brand photos exported from the original site,
  resized for the web.
- `icon.svg` — hub icon.
